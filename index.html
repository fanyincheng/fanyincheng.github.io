About Me

I am a Ph.D. Student in the Department of Speech, Language, and Hearing Sciences at The University of Texas at Austin.

I was born in Taichung, Taiwan. I received my M.A. Degree in Speech, Language, and Hearing Sciences at University of Colorado at Boulder in 2016. My research interests include evaluating the underlying mechanisms in auditory processing at subcortical and cortical levels. I am currently using non-invasive electrophysiological methods to investigate how the auditory system encodes and decodes successive sounds including speech.
